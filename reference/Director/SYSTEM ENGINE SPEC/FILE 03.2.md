# 📦 SEEDANCE 2.0 SYSTEM ENGINE SPEC

## 🧠 FILE 03.2 — DIRECTOR AI SYSTEM（导演人格系统）

---

# 🎬 1. 系统定位（System Purpose）

Director AI System 是 Seedance 2.0 的“导演决策层”。

> 它负责决定：
>
> * 这个故事应该怎么拍
> * 摄影机应该怎么看
> * 情绪应该如何推进
> * 节奏应该如何控制
> * 整部作品应该具备什么导演风格

---

## 🧠 核心定义

```text id="da01"
Story
→ Director Personality Selection
→ Visual Philosophy
→ Camera Philosophy
→ Pacing Philosophy
→ Emotional Strategy
→ Director-Controlled Storyboard
```

# 🎬 3. Director AI Core Stack

---

## Layer 1 — Visual Philosophy

定义：

```text id="vp01"
How the director sees the world.
```

决定：

* 现实主义
* 超现实主义
* 诗意表达
* 极简主义
* 商业化表达

---

## Layer 2 — Camera Philosophy

定义：

```text id="cp01"
How the camera behaves emotionally.
```

决定：

* 静止观察
* 情绪参与
* 第一视角沉浸
* 全知旁观者

---

## Layer 3 — Narrative Philosophy

定义：

```text id="np01"
How information is revealed.
```

决定：

* 直接叙事
* 延迟叙事
* 谜题叙事
* 情绪叙事

# 🎥 4. Camera Personality Engine

---

## 📦 CAMERA BEHAVIOR TYPES

### Observer Director

```text id="cam01"
Camera observes.
Never interferes.
```

特征：

* static
* wide shot
* long take

---

### Emotional Director

```text id="cam02"
Camera feels with character.
```

特征：

* close-up
* dolly-in
* emotional framing

---

### Immersive Director

```text id="cam03"
Camera becomes the audience.
```

特征：

* POV
* handheld
* subjective movement

---

### Epic Director

```text id="cam04"
Camera dominates space.
```

特征：

* drone
* crane
* large-scale movement

# 🚨 12. Hard Constraints

---

## ❌ Forbidden

* Director style changes mid-sequence
* Conflicting visual philosophies
* Multiple pacing systems simultaneously
* Camera behavior without director logic

---

## ✅ Required

* One dominant director profile
* Consistent visual philosophy
* Consistent camera philosophy
* Consistent pacing philosophy
* Consistent emotional strategy
